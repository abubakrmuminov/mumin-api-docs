Our API provides access to thousands of authentic Hadiths from major collections like **Sahih al-Bukhari**, **Sahih Muslim**, and more. We offer translations in multiple languages, including **English**, **Russian**, **Turkish**, and **Arabic**.

## Why Mumin API?

- **Authenticity**: Carefully curated data from trusted sources.
- **Multilingual**: Access hadiths in your preferred language.
- **Performance**: Fast globally distributed API.
- **Developer Friendly**: Excellent documentation, SDKs, and community support.
- **Free to Start**: Generous free tier for developers and students.

## What can you build?

- Mobile apps for daily hadiths.
- Websites for searching and reading.
- Telegram/Discord bots.
- Widgets for your blog or masjid website.

## Base URL

All API requests should be made to:

```
https://api.hadith.mumin.ink/v1
```
